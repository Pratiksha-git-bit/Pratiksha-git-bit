FraudShield Agent
AI · Real-time fraud detection
Problem

Fraud ops analysts are overwhelmed by alert volume, most of it false positives, from rule-based systems that flag transactions without explaining why. That forces manual investigation on every alert, slows response time, and drives up operational cost — while a missed fraud event still carries real financial and reputational risk.

An AI-powered fraud detection system for internal fraud operations analysts. An XGBoost classifier scores transactions in real time; a LangGraph multi-agent layer — Document Verification, Risk Scoring, Compliance, and Case Notes agents — routes the highest-risk cases to a human analyst, with a LangChain/FAISS RAG layer grounding every explanation in transaction metadata so it doesn't hallucinate a reason for the flag.

ShopEase
Checkout conversion diagnostics
Problem

ShopEase saw a sharp rise in cart abandonment over three months — customers were adding products to cart but dropping off just before payment completion. That drop-off was hitting revenue, retention, and platform growth directly, and the underlying friction points in the checkout flow hadn't been diagnosed yet.

A diagnostic and redesign of an e-commerce checkout flow losing customers just before payment. The work moved from business questions ("where exactly are we losing people, and on which payment method?") to a prioritized set of fixes — guest checkout, single-page flow, payment retry — backed by a BRD/PRD, funnel data, and Figma wireframes.
